About आसन.ai

Hello ! Welcome to आसन.ai, A platform developed to serve as your Yoga Companion.

We developed this model inspired by the YMCA model created by Daniel Shiffman , where he worked on recognising YMCA pattern from the YMCA Dance.

The model uses Pose.Net, developed by Google’s TensorFlow, and ml5.js, a JavaScript library for Machine Learning on the browser.

A major part of our learning came from 3 Blue 1 Brown's playlist on neural network and The Coding Trains playlist on Machine Learning.
